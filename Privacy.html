Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 7, 2025

Welcome to SepticSense. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our tools, or request our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in a few different ways to provide and improve our services.

A. Information You Provide to Us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out a contact or inquiry form: This may include your first and last name, email address, phone number, and any message you send.
  • Request emergency service: This includes your name, phone number, property address, and details about the emergency.
  • Submit a technical support or partnership form: This may include your name, email, company name, and details of your request.
  • Upload files: If you use the photo upload feature, we will collect the images and any metadata associated with them.

B. Information We Collect Automatically

When you navigate our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, including:

  • Log and Usage Data: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, referral URLs, pages viewed, and the dates/times of your visits.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to help operate our site, remember your preferences, and understand how our services are used. For more details, see the "Cookies" section below.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To Provide and Manage Our Services: To respond to your inquiries, schedule consultations, dispatch emergency services, and provide technical support.
  • To Communicate With You: To send you service-related communications, updates, and promotional materials that may be of interest to you. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • To Improve Our Website: To analyze usage trends and user behavior to enhance our website, tools, and overall user experience.
  • For Security and Legal Compliance: To protect our website and users from fraud, to enforce our Terms of Service, and to comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as website hosting, data analysis, and email delivery.
  • With Local Partners and Contractors: To fulfill a service request or emergency call, we may share your contact details and service needs with a certified, local septic professional in our network.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid request by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).
  • During Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small data files stored on your browser. We use them to help our site function, analyze performance, and personalize your experience. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our website.

5. International Data Transfers

SepticSense operates in the United States, Canada, and Europe. Your information may be processed in a country other than your own, including the United States, where data protection laws may differ. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

For residents of the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK: You have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to restrict processing.

For residents of California: You have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@septicsense.com. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law.

7. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please do not hesitate to contact us:

Email: privacy@septicsense.com

Via our website: Contact Form
