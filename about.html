About SepticSense

Leading septic system experts providing professional maintenance solutions and educational resources since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a team of environmental engineers and septic system specialists, SepticSense emerged from a simple mission: make professional septic system maintenance accessible to every homeowner and RV owner.

Over 15 years, we've helped thousands of property owners save money, prevent environmental damage, and maintain healthy septic systems through our comprehensive tools, guides, and expert consultation services.

Today, we serve customers across the USA, Canada, and Europe, combining traditional septic expertise with modern technology to deliver unmatched value and peace of mind.
